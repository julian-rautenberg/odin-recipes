Lasagna

Description

This is the best lasagna we have ever had, including restaurants! Great with a salad and garlic bread.

Per Serving: 398 calories; protein 23.4g; carbohydrates 39.7g; fat 17.1g; cholesterol 57.8mg; sodium 1168.7mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 3 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 5 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
